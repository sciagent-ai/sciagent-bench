## Three-Zone TiO₂/N-BK7 Metasurface In-Coupler — S4 RCWA Reproduction

### ✅ MFE Verification: **26.6% ≥ 25.3%** (paper target) — PASS

---

### Method Summary

The simulation follows the paper's **light-reversibility approach**: instead of simulating an air-incident beam diffracting into N-BK7, we inject a TE-polarized plane wave from inside the N-BK7 substrate at angles θ_glass ∈ [41.1°, 62.5°], which correspond to external XFOV angles -10° to +10° via the grating equation:

```
sin(θ_glass) = sin(θ_ext)/n_NBK7 + λ/(n_NBK7 · d)
```

At each angle, S4 RCWA (NumBasis=121) returns:
- **η_T₋₁**: power in the −1 transmitted order (= first-order diffraction into air from air side)
- **η_R₀**: zeroth-order reflected power back into glass

---
